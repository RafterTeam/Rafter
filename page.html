This game was created to show It's players some of the issues faced by people who are homeless, and the ways that Shelter, the charity it was made for can help somebody in that situation. To help Shelter to continue their work, Please donate to them here: Donate To Shelter

Our game is called Homeless it is a top down retro game about being homeless. The goal of the game is to find Shelter so you can get a new home. There are multiple places in the game that can help you on your journey such as Shops, Hospitals and Hotels these building are interactive and will give options when you select them. The stats for your character are Health, Hunger and Money.

You are Homeless. Your house was destroyed in a storm and you have no money because you lost your job the week before, you now have to live on the streets to fend for yourself. Find support from everyone you can, you must get shelter.
